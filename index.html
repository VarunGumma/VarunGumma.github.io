Hello,

I am a SCAI Center Fellow with Kalika Bali (formerly a Research Intern with Sunayana Sitaram) at Microsoft Research India. Previously, I was a Graduate Research Assistant at AI4Bharat where I was advised by Raj Dabre, Pratyush Kumar and Mitesh Khapra. I am broadly interested in the topics of Multilingualism and Efficient Natural Language Processing (NLP). My focus lies specifically in the areas of Knowledge Distillation, Model Compression and Efficient Training methods for Multilingual Neural Machine Translation Models (MNMT). As we witness the rise of Large Language Models (LLMs), I am interesting in evaluating LLMs as evaluators.

I have been fortunate to work with an amazing set of researchers in the past. During the summer of 2022, I interned at the National University of Singapore with Aditya Karanam. My bachelor thesis at BITS Pilani, Hyderabad was under the guidance of NL Bhanu Murthy. During my pre-final year semester project at BITS Hyderabad, I had the opportunity to work with Barsha Mitra and Soumyadeep Dey.

Teaching is a passion of mine, and I have actively contributed as a Teaching Assistant for various courses. Ranging from Deep Learning, Linear Algebra and Random Processes, Machine Learning, Foundations of Data Science, Data Structures and Algorithms, Discrete Mathematics, Computer Programming, to Introductory Physics, I have engaged with students across both my graduate and undergraduate years.

I graduated with a B.E in Computer Science & Engineering and a Minor in Physics from BITS Pilani, Hyderabad, India in 2021, and subsequently earned my Masters from IIT Madras in 2023. For more details, check my CV or hit me up on my email.
